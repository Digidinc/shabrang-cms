---# The Digital vs. Analog War

Stephen Wolfram proposes the ultimate **Digital Physics**: the universe is code.
FRC proposes the ultimate **Analog Physics**: the universe is music.

In a digital computer (Wolfram), states are 0 or 1. Errors are fatal.
In an analog computer (FRC), states are phases $\phi$. Errors are noise, which can be filtered.

Biology is messy, wet, and robust. It looks much more like a Resonance Field than a Hypergraph. If we want to build AGI that feels alive, we should bet on the Analog.
