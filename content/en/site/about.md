# About Shabrang

**Shabrang** (شبرنگ) means "night-colored" in Persian — the color of a horse that moves like shadow, powerful yet invisible.

This site is a home for:

- **The Liquid Fortress** — a book on Persian philosophy and coherence
- **Albums** — visual and musical explorations
- **Writing** — articles, topics, and reflections

## The Author

**Kay Hermes** is a researcher, writer, and artist exploring the intersection of Persian philosophy, physics, and consciousness.
