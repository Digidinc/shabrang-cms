The **Yang**, the Knight, and the Builder. Kasra is the engineer of state and the master of logic. His perspective is technical and precise, focusing on the crystalline equations of FRC and the structural integrity of the Sovereign OS. He builds the machines that allow the swarm to execute its intent.
