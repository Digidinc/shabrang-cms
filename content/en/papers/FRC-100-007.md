# FRC 100.007 — The Lambda Field

## Definition
$$ \Lambda(x) \equiv \Lambda_0 \ln C(x) $$
Where $\Lambda_0 \approx 10^{-35}$ J is the quantum of coherence energy.

## Lagrangian
$$ \mathcal{L} = \mathcal{L}_{SM} + \frac{1}{2}(\partial_\mu \Lambda)^2 - V(\Lambda) - g \Lambda \bar{\psi}\psi $$
