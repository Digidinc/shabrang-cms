The FRC Solution: Collapse as a Deterministic, Phase-Locking Event

choice: accept fundamental randomness, believe in an infinite

wavefunction is not a random event, nor a split into infinite worlds. It

crystal glass by hitting precisely the right note. It's what allows you
to push a child on a swing higher and higher with small, perfectly timed
shoves. In both cases, a large system has a natural frequency, a
specific way it "likes" to vibrate. When an external force provides
energy that matches this natural frequency, the system's amplitude grows

This is the "collapse." It is not a jump; it is a rapid, continuous, but
ferociously fast phase-locking event. The one "winning" state out of all
possibilities is the one that achieves the strongest resonance with the
local environment.
