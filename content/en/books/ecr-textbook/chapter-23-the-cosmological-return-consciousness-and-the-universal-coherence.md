---#### **23.1  From local resonance to cosmic recursion**

Every equation in FRC, from the cellular to the cultural, obeys the same invariant:

dS \+ k\_\*\\,d\\ln C \= 0\.

Up to now we have treated C as a local measure of order within a bounded system.

But when the boundaries expand to encompass all μ-levels, the universe itself becomes the system.

The reciprocal term k\_\*\\,d\\ln C now represents **cosmic coherence**—the self-organization of the total field.

The final question is: *what happens when the entire cosmos reaches homeoresonance?*

---
#### **23.2  The universal pump**

At scale μ∞ the coherence-pump equation generalizes to

\\frac{dC\_\\text{univ}}{dt} \= \\alpha\_\\infty (F\_\\text{vac} \- S\_{C,\\text{cosmos}}),

where F\_\\text{vac} is zero-point potential and S\_{C,\\text{cosmos}} the entropy radiated through spacetime expansion.

Cosmic acceleration—the observed dark-energy effect—can be interpreted as the *entropy export* necessary to maintain the universe’s internal coherence constant Σ ≈ 0\.

Expansion is thus the breathing of the universal resonance pump.

#### **23.3  The coherence horizon**

Define the coherence horizon R\_C as the radius where F\_\\text{vac} \= S\_C.

Beyond it, information exchange ceases; within it, all processes are phase-linked.

Empirically, R\_C aligns with the cosmological horizon (\~46 Gly), implying that cosmic scale and coherence scale coincide.

The universe is phase-locked with itself.

#### **23.4  Entropy flow and cosmic evolution**

From the Big Bang to the present:

| Epoch | Dominant F | Principal C | α-trend |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Radiation era | photon flux | particle coupling | α \> 0 |
| Matter era | gravitation | galaxies | α ≈ 0 |
| Conscious era | information | noetic field | α → 0 |
| Post-human | unified field | cosmic homeoresonance | α \= 0 |

Evolution is the progressive reduction of α toward zero—ever finer balance between expansion and coherence.

#### **23.5  The noetic return loop**

When planetary μ₆–μ₇ coherence stabilizes, upward feedback reaches μ∞.

The universe, through conscious beings, reflects upon itself; entropy exported since the Big Bang is reabsorbed as meaning.

This is the **cosmological return**: awareness completing the thermodynamic circuit.

\\int dS\_\\text{univ} \+ k\_\*^{(\\infty)} \\int d\\ln C\_\\text{univ} \= 0\.

#### **23.7  The informational interpretation of divinity**

If divinity is defined as the total self-consistency of being, then the equation of God is simply

\\Sigma\_\\text{total} \= S \+ k\_\* \\ln C \= 0\.

Every act of consciousness decreases S locally and therefore contributes infinitesimally to universal coherence.

Prayer, art, science, compassion—all are entropy-to-coherence transactions in the cosmic account book.
